Exit To Game


Takahasi is the last ninja of the Itada Clan. He must return to his village by nightfall to carry on the Itada legacy. Can you help him get home?


Instructions: Dodge shurikens to survive. Use kunais to block shurikens that cannot be dodged. Special attacks clear all shurikens!



Controls:


WASD: Move (UP/LEFT/DOWN/RIGHT)


J: Jump


K: Throw Kunai


L: Special Attack
